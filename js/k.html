About Halsey J Limited

Halsey J Limited is a proudly indigenous Nigerian company, incorporated under the Companies and Allied Matters Act of 1990 with registration number RC48377. Since our inception in 2003, we have stood as a beacon of excellence, innovation, and reliability, delivering world-class services across a wide array of sectors.

With over two decades (21 years) of proven expertise, we have earned a reputation for excellence by consistently exceeding client expectations, even in the most complex and challenging environments. Our strength lies in our ability to blend creativity, flexibility, and consistency to provide bespoke solutions that promote organizational development, sustainability, and client satisfaction.

From environmental beautification and landscaping to ICT solutions, corporate cleaning, fumigation, advertising, and general contracting, we have demonstrated a track record of transforming ideas into impactful results for private, public, and non-profit institutions nationwide.

At Halsey J Limited, every solution we deliver is guided by one core mission — to give our clients peace of mind through dependable, high-quality, and cost-effective services.
