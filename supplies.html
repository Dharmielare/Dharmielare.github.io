OUR PROCESS

Our Procurement Process

We follow a streamlined procurement process to ensure efficiency and satisfaction

01

Needs Assessment

We begin by understanding your specific requirements, budget constraints, and timeline. This helps us tailor our procurement strategy to meet your unique needs.

02

Sourcing & Selection

Our procurement team identifies the best suppliers and products that meet your quality standards and budget requirements, leveraging our extensive network of trusted partners.

03

Quotation & Approval

We provide detailed quotations for your review and approval, ensuring transparency in pricing and specifications before proceeding with the procurement.

04

Order Processing

Once approved, we place orders with suppliers, manage the purchasing process, and track the status of your order from start to finish.

05

Delivery & Installation

We coordinate the delivery of your supplies and, when needed, provide installation services to ensure everything is set up correctly and ready for use.

06

Follow-up & Support

We conduct follow-up checks to ensure your satisfaction with the delivered supplies and provide ongoing support for any issues or additional requirements.
